Frequently Asked Questions

This post was written by Jordan.

General

Q: Why did you make a website?

A: I initially made a website to complete an assignment for my Web Design I course in the fall of 2017.

Q: What is your goal with this website?

A: The goal is to display work that I am proud of, and motivate me to do work worthy of sharing.

Q: What type of work are you going to display?

A: Ideally I am going to display websites I’ve designed for local businesses, as well as the story behind those sites. However, I am not going to limit this website to just that type of work. In short, I am going to display whatever I’m proud of.

Donations

Q: Why do you give visitors so many opportunities to donate to you?

A: At first I did it because I thought it was funny, and unique for a portfolio website. I left it because I legitimately do not want to keep paying out of pocket to keep a .com domain name. However, I also wanted to give people the chance to keep the site afloat. In other words, I wanted to see if I could follow the Wikipedia model, and create something that people liked enough to donate.

Q: What are you going to do with the donations?

A: I am going to use all of the donations to pay for the domain hosting fees.

Q: Has anyone donated yet?

A: No, but if and when people do, I will update this answer if they want to be recognized.
